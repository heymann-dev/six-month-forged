# Project Northstar v30 Reward Architecture

The application contains 19 deterministic earned rewards: nine early-week gates and ten five-level Summit gates.

Exact reward contents remain sealed in the interface until the applicable evidence gate and integrity confirmation are satisfied. The system is not random and is not gambling-based.

The catalog was verified against Hudson's priorities:

- Alternating Kaiser Roseville night-shift rotation
- Protected sleep and true off-day claim windows
- Current knee, ankle, Lisfranc, and rehabilitation restrictions
- Physique and upper-body development
- Reading, prayer, attention control, and real-world connection
- Return-to-Mountains preparation
- ICU and professional advancement
- The $100,000 cash-reserve target
- The $1,200 commitment to Dad

Every reward has a category, rarity, schedule-safe claim window, and spending ceiling. A revealed reward may be exchanged for an equally healthy and financially responsible alternative.
